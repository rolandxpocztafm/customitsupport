Our Services

Comprehensive CAD and engineering solutions tailored to your product development needs.

3D CAD Modeling

Precision 3D modeling services for parts and assemblies using industry-leading CAD software including SolidWorks, Inventor, and Solid Edge.

Technical Drawing

Production-ready technical drawings with GD&T (Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing) following ASME Y14.5 standards.

Prototyping Support

Design optimization for various prototyping methods including 3D printing, CNC machining, and injection molding.

Assembly Design

Complex assembly design with motion studies, interference checks, and BOM (Bill of Materials) generation.

Reverse Engineering

3D scanning and CAD recreation of existing parts with dimensional verification and material analysis.

Design Optimization

DFM (Design for Manufacturing) analysis to reduce production costs while maintaining product functionality.
